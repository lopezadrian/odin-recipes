Fajitas

"Fajitas"by spurekar is licensed under CC BY 2.0

Description

This classic dish found in Tex-Mex restaurants and beyond is easy-to-make and guilt-free. If you want a quick meal that's filling but light on the wallet, look no further. All you need a couple of cheap ingredients, a single pan, and plenty of tortillas.

As a college student, fajitas were as convenient as ramen or chicken and rice, but I've found them to be not as common. Hopefully this simple recipe helps to convince you to try it out for yourself and add it to your repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chicken breast (but you can use your meat of choice, or portobello mushrooms as a vegan option)
  • 1 Green bell pepper
  • 1 Red bell pepper
  • 1 White onion
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Cumin
  • Flour or corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breast, bell peppers and onion into strips
  2. Season chicken with salt, black pepper, and cumin to taste
  3. In a pan with a high heat oil, turn heat on high
  4. Put onions in the pan and saute until slightly transparent
  5. Add bell peppers and chicken and lightly add more seasoning
  6. Take off heat and warm tortillas on a skillet
  7. Serve fajitas on the pan and into tortillas like a taco
  8. Enjoy!
